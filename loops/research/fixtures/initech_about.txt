<h1>About Initech</h1>
<p>
  Initech was founded in 2011 in Austin, Texas. We build workflow automation
  software for mid-market manufacturers. As of 2026 we employ about 480 people
  across offices in Austin, Berlin, and Singapore.
</p>
<p>
  Initech is privately held. In 2024 we raised a $60 million Series C led by
  Bluth Capital. We have not announced any plans to go public.
</p>
<p>
  Our flagship product, FlowForge, is used by over 3,000 factories worldwide.
  We are SOC 2 Type II certified and ISO 27001 certified.
</p>
